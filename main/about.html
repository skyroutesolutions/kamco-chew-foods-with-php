History

Who we are

Our story started in 1998, we were a manufacturer of bubble gum and chewing gum While acquiring Indian mass’s gum consumptions, Kamco expanded and came to produce all kind of confectionery items such as hollow gum, bubble gum, chewing gum, centre filled gums, chocolate gems, chocolate beans, chocolate paste, moulded chocolate, centre filled biscuits & Choco layered biscuit balls, hard candy & lollipops, toffee, cake, Choco enrobed & cream wafers, surprise toys & Khazana items, spicy and fruit flavoured balls, jelly cup and pop, imli (tamarind) paste, soft drink powder etc. also producing gum base. We are very privileged to have not only made our mark in India but has now exported to countries like south Africa, UAE, Saudi Arab, Lebanon, Nepal, South America and Cambodia.
